Are ICO's Legal?

ICOs are perfectly legal given all the documentation is in a proper place. In most cases, as long as the company conducting the Initial Coin Offering does not misrepresent their coin as a financial security, representation of company ownership and investors follow the local laws of their own nation regarding reporting of income, it is indeed legal for everyone involved in the ICO development and launch process.

Coin Creation and
Blockchain Development

In a very short span of time, Cryptocurrencies have gained immense momentum and their well- deserved popularity has caused the value of Bitcoins and Altcoins to skyrocket in recent times.

Cryptocurrency is a digital asset designed to work as a medium of exchange using cryptography to secure the transactions and control the creation of additional units of the currency. Bitcoin is the world’s biggest and first decentralized ledger currency which has received a massive success with a remarkable response from the global communities.

With over 1700 cryptocurrencies as of August 2018, the total market capitalization of cryptocurrencies stood over US$ 270bn.

Cryptocurrencies have opened various avenues for multiple entities, from investors to governments, to explore. New startups have been able to raise billions of dollars through Initial Coin Offerings (ICOs).

My Deposit To Bitrockz Account Is Not Confirmed, Why?

Blockchain transactions are not instant and require verification via 'Confirmations'. The Confirmations occur when transactions are confirmed to be legitimate by miners. This confirmed block will be added to the Blockchain and continues to be confirmed as new blocks are added. We are not performing these Confirmations or have any control over the time in which these Confirmations are completed. The Confirmations are completed by miners on the Blockchain. Transactions with higher fees will are far more likely to be added to a block first.
